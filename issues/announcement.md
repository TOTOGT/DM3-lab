## Why AXLE?

The fruit fly 🪰 is our toy machine — small enough to simulate completely, complex enough to instantiate the full operator chain `C → K → F → U`. Starting from the fly connectome, the same operator algebra reappears in:

- Saturn's 40-year polar hexagon (best physical instantiation)
- Magnetic reconnection in plasma sheets
- Calabi-Yau compactification in string theory
- Collatz orbits (sharpest mathematical test case)
- Martian colony closed-loop life support

Not as analogy. As exact morphisms in the category **dm³**.
